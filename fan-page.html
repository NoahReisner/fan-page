Evolution of the franchise!

The franchise first appeared on the PS1. Although The franchise did not become a hit until it appeared on the PS2 with Persona 3. Persona 3 the first game in the franchise to include the social link system. The addition of that system made persona the franchise it is today.

Explanation of the Social Link System

The social link system is very hard to explain. So here is a list of what it does.

  • Gives more character devolopment to supporting characters.
  • Gives supporting characters support skills for use in battles.
  • Allows for high level personas in persona fusion.
  • In some case provides extra ending content to be unlocked.
